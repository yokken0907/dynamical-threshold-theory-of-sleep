# Claim Boundary

## Allowed claims

- This repository accompanies an AI-assisted independent research manuscript on DTTS.
- DTTS is a mathematical and computational prototype for sleep-like recovery phase transitions.
- The model represents a multicellular biological system as an adaptive oscillator network with local load, sleep propensity, and protected interactions.
- Numerical proof-of-concept simulations reproduce qualitative motifs: heterogeneous load accumulation, growth of sleep propensity, bounded downscaling of coupling, and preferential preservation of protected edges.

## Forbidden claims

- This is a clinical sleep model.
- This diagnoses or treats sleep disorders.
- This has been validated on human or animal sleep data.
- This proves the biological function of sleep.
- This provides medical advice.
- This predicts individual sleep states.
- This is a full neuroscientific model of the brain.

## Standard short wording

> DTTS is a mathematical network prototype for sleep-like recovery phase transitions. It is not a clinical sleep model, medical tool, human-data validation, or complete biological theory of sleep.
