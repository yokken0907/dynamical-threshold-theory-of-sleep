# Limitations

## Model limitations

- The model is a prototype network theory, not a biological brain model.
- Nodes are abstract oscillatory elements, not anatomically identified neurons or brain regions.
- The use-dependent drive is synthetic.
- The circadian term is a simplified gating function.
- The protection field is an abstract coherence-history variable.
- The free-energy formulation is a reduced mathematical closure, not a thermodynamic measurement of biological sleep.

## Validation limitations

- The simulations are proof-of-concept only.
- No human EEG, local field potential, calcium imaging, or animal sleep dataset is fitted.
- No clinical endpoint is predicted.
- No sleep disorder is diagnosed or treated.
- The results show qualitative consistency with selected theoretical motifs, not empirical validation.

## Repository limitations

- The original uploaded folder contained a large Python virtual environment, which is excluded from the GitHub body.
- This repository is a paper companion archive, not a full execution environment.
